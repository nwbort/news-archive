    Celine Cremer

    Remains have been found in the area where Celine Cremer disappeared in Tasmania.

    The Belgian backpacker has been missing since June 2023.

    In December, her phone was found in an area near Philosopher Falls.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Nipah virus

    Asian countries are stepping up screening measures after cases of Nipah virus were detected in India. 

    The illness has a 40 to 75 per cent fatality rate in humans. 

    Nipah virus generally spreads from animals to humans but can spread between close contacts. 

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Uni courses

    Health is the the most popular area of study for Australian university students this year.

    Engineering courses recorded the biggest jump when compared with last year.

    However, authorities are monitoring a decline in students taking up IT courses.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Heatwave

    Australia has sweltered through a massive heatwave.

    Temperatures were up to 20C above averages in parts of the country.

    In some areas, the mercury neared 50C.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Shark victim

    Nico Antic, who was bitten by a shark, has died in hospital.

    Authorities believe the 12-year-old was attacked by a bull shark while jumping off a rock ledge at Vaucluse in Sydney. 

    Water conditions and splashing may have created the perfect environment for the attack.
