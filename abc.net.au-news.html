Everyone's Talking About...

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Robert Irwin

    Robert Irwin has won the American version of Dancing with the Stars.

    His win comes 10 years after his sister, Bindi, won her season of the celebrity dance show.

    Irwin went into the final with a rib injury and was up against four other celebrities and their partners.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Formula 1

    Oscar Piastri and Lando Norris have both been disqualified from the Las Vegas Grand Prix.

    The skid blocks on both cars ended the race below the minimum thickness of 9mm.

    McLaren has since apologised to its drivers, saying the breach was unintentional.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    ARIA awards

    Amyl & The Sniffers have dominated at the ARIAs taking out four awards.

    The Melbourne band won Album of the Year, Best Group, Best Rock Album, and Best Cover Art.

    Producer and songwriter Ninajirachi won three ARIAs, including for Breakthrough Artist and Best Solo Artist.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Epstein vote

    A bill requiring the release of files related to Jeffrey Epstein has passed US congress.

    Both houses voted to release unclassified documents related to the dead sex offender.

    Donald Trump supported the vote, despite previously arguing against it.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Sussan Ley

    Opposition Leader Sussan Ley says she guarantees she will lead the Coalition at the next election.

    Ms Ley's leadership has been under threat in recent weeks.

    Reports have emerged that Andrew Hastie and Angus Taylor may be potential challengers.
