    Albanese and Trump

    Anthony Albanese and Donald Trump have signed a landmark critical minerals deal.  

    The deal, worth $US8.5 billion ($13 billion), is essentially an agreement to invest in projects in Australia. 

    Mr Trump says the AUKUS nuclear submarine deal will continue. 

  • Slide 1 of 3

    The Louvre heist

    Thieves have broken into the Louvre Museum in Paris and stolen priceless jewellery.

    The robbers are still on the run after using a crane to break into the museum in broad daylight.

    The heist took just minutes and was carried out 250 metres from the Mona Lisa.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Prince Andrew

    Prince Andrew is giving up his royal titles and honours after increased media scrutiny.   

    The prince will no longer be referred to as his royal highness, nor the duke of York.   

    The prince had already scaled back his duties but has now gone a step further. 

  • Slide 1 of 3

    ADF rollover

    A soldier has died after a single-vehicle rollover at a Townsville training course.

    The man was identified Tulsa Rumney.  

    Defence Minister Richard Marles says the fleet involved will be suspended amid investigations. 

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Ariarne Titmus retires

    Four-time Olympian Ariarne Titmus has announced her retirement at the age of 25. 

    Titmus says it is tough to retire but she is comfortable with the decision.   

    Titmus will finish her career as one of the greatest distance swimmers of all time.
