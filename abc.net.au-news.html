Everyone's Talking About...

  • Slide 1 of 3

    BOM website

    The Bureau of Meteorology (BOM) has been asked to fix its new $4 million website.

    It comes after a torrent of complaints by users finding the site difficult to navigate.       

    The BOM says it will respond to the feedback. 

  • Slide 1 of 3

    The Louvre arrests

    French police have arrested two people suspected of breaking into the Louvre. 

    The men from Paris are known to police with one of the suspects arrested at Charles de Gaulle Airport.

    There is no indication if the jewels worth $156 million have been recovered. 

  • Slide 1 of 3

    NBA arrests

    NBA coach Chauncey Billups and player Terry Rozier were arrested for their involvement in sports betting and poker games.

    Billups was charged with participating in a conspiracy to fix high-stakes card games backed by crime families.

    Rozier is accused in a separate scheme of concocting fraudulent bets by exploiting insider NBA information.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Microplastics

    Homes are full of hidden microplastics that may be harming our health.    

    There is more than 200 times the volume of plastics in our lives compared to 75 years ago.    

    Nanoplastics can be absorbed into the bloodstream but solid evidence of human harm is still unclear.          

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Albanese and Trump

    Anthony Albanese and Donald Trump have signed a landmark critical minerals deal.  

    The deal, worth $US8.5 billion ($13 billion), is essentially an agreement to invest in projects in Australia. 

    Mr Trump says the AUKUS nuclear submarine deal will continue.
