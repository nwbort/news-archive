    Shark victim

    Nico Antic, who was bitten by a shark almost a week ago, has died in hospital.

    Water conditions and splashing may have created the perfect environment for the attack.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Aurora australis

    An impressive aurora australis has been seen in many parts of Australia.

    The light show was caused by a severe solar storm hitting the Earth.

    Experts say the chance to witness auroras in the coming years may decline as the Sun enters a new phase. 

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Valentino

    Powerhouse Italian fashion designer Valentino Garavani has died at the age of 93.   

    Known for his Valentino red, he was an enduring force in fashion for more than half a century.     

    Valentino became the first choice for royals, first ladies and film stars.   

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Adelaide Writers' Week

    Adelaide Writers' Week has been cancelled after a week of escalating controversy. 

    The Adelaide Festival board rescinded the invitation to Palestinian Australian author Randa Abdel-Fattah.

    More than 180 writers pulled out of the event.
