    Bondi attack

    Fifteen people have been killed after two men opened fire in Bondi Beach.

    One of the gunmen was shot and killed by police and the other is under police guard in hospital.

    The PM says gun laws across the nation will be strengthened.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    US travel requirements

    The US plans to require tourists from more nations to hand over five years of their social media history.

    Applicants could also be asked to supply all phone numbers they have used in the past five years.

    It means Australians may face longer delays and an unprecedented invasion of privacy.

  • Slide 1 of 4

    Bonnie Blue

    Adult star Bonnie Blue has been deported from Indonesia.

    A racy stunt she planned in Bali attracted the attention of authorities.

    The 'bang bus' she drove around in was found to have violated road rules.

    She was fined $20 and deported.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Social ban

    Australia's social media ban for teenagers is now in effect.

    Platforms are required to ban under-16s from holding accounts.

    Instagram, Snapchat and TikTok are all targeted in the ban.
