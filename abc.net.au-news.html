Everyone's Talking About...

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Coral Adventurer

    The cruise ship was carrying 80 tourists to remote PNG when it ran aground on Saturday.

    Inspections of the vessel have yet to find any major damage.

    By Tuesday, passengers had been flown to Cairns, and a tugboat helped refloat the ship.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Bondi hero speaks

    Bondi Beach hero Ahmed Al Ahmed says he felt "a power in my body, my brain" before he disarmed a terrorist at a Hanukkah festival.

    Fifteen people died in the attack, but Mr Ahmeds actions are believed to have saved countless" others.

    Of his decision to intervene, he said: "My soul asked me to do that."

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Courtesy waves

    The ABC travelled more than 550km to see if drivers were still giving out courtesy waves.

    Locals and truck drivers say the friendly gesture is declining along highways, but is more common on smaller back roads.

    A road safety advocate is hoping to introduce a National Wave Day in 2026.

  • Slide 1 of 3

    Battle of sexes

    Nick Kyrgios has defeated women's world number one Aryna Sabalenka in the latest "battle of the sexes" exhibition match.

    The spectacle, marketed under the same banner as the 1973 match between Billie Jean King and Bobby Riggs, was widely criticised.

    Kyrgios won in straight sets 6-3, 6-3 in front of a sold-out crowd in Dubai.
